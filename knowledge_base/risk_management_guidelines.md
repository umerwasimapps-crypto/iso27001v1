# Risk Management Guidelines (Demo)

## ISO 27005 Alignment
- Use asset-threat-vulnerability triads and qualitative scales (Low/Medium/High).
- Document risk acceptance criteria approved by leadership.

## Treatment Strategies
- Mitigate: implement controls (e.g., PAM, encryption) with owners and timelines.
- Transfer: contracts/insurance for shared responsibility.
- Avoid: alter processes/services to remove risk exposure.
- Accept: document rationale, monitor periodically.

## Continuous Monitoring
- Review high risks quarterly; medium annually.
- Feed incident/vulnerability data back into the register.
- Map ISO controls to other frameworks (NIST AC, AU, CM, CP, IR) to support dual compliance.
