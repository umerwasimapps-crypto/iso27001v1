# ISO/IEC 27001 Clause Overview (Demo)

## Clause 4 – Context of the Organization
- Understand internal/external issues impacting the ISMS.
- Identify interested parties and define the ISMS scope.

## Clause 5 – Leadership
- Top management sets policy, assigns roles, and aligns objectives with business goals.

## Clause 6 – Planning
- Plan for risks and opportunities; define measurable security objectives.

## Clause 7 – Support
- Provide resources, competency, awareness, communication, and documented information.

## Clause 8 – Operation
- Implement and control ISMS processes; perform risk assessments and treatments.

## Clause 9 – Performance Evaluation
- Monitor, measure, analyze, and evaluate the ISMS; run internal audits and management reviews.

## Clause 10 – Improvement
- Manage nonconformities and drive continual improvement.
