The Information Asset Register should be maintained as a living document with regular reviews (at least annually or when significant changes occur). It should be updated when new assets are acquired, existing assets are modified or decommissioned, or when classification requirements change. The register should be accessible to authorized personnel and integrated with the risk management process to ensure appropriate controls are applied based on the classification. Regular audits should verify the accuracy and completeness of the register entries. Ownership of assets should be clearly assigned and documented, with escalation paths defined for addressing security issues. The register should also be aligned with the organization's risk assessment process to ensure that assets are properly protected according to their business importance and sensitivity classifications.
