## Monitoring and Follow-up Narrative

The risk treatment plan will be monitored through a structured framework that ensures continuous effectiveness and alignment with ISO 27001 requirements. High-priority risks (R-001 to R-004) will undergo **quarterly reviews** with detailed progress tracking, while medium-priority risks (R-005 to R-012) will be reviewed **semi-annually**. Each treatment will have **monthly status reports** submitted to the relevant business leadership, with escalation paths defined for delays or implementation challenges.

**Monitoring Activities:**
- **Control Effectiveness Testing**: Quarterly validation of implemented controls through penetration testing and vulnerability assessments
- **Residual Risk Assessment**: Monthly evaluation of remaining risk levels after treatment implementation
- **Compliance Verification**: Bi-monthly checks against ISO 27001 Annex A requirements and organizational policies
- **Stakeholder Communication**: Monthly progress updates to Interested Parties with tailored messaging for each stakeholder group

**Follow-up Processes:**
- **Post-Implementation Reviews**: Comprehensive evaluation 30 days after each treatment completion to measure effectiveness
- **Continuous Improvement**: Integration of lessons learned into ongoing ISMS processes and control optimization
- **Change Management**: Formal change control process for any modifications to implemented treatments
- **Documentation Maintenance**: Regular updates to risk treatment documentation and control evidence

**Escalation Mechanisms:**
- **Tier 1**: Treatment owners resolve issues within 5 business days
- **Tier 2**: Security Operations escalates to IT Operations for cross-functional issues
- **Tier 3**: Business Leadership involvement for strategic or resource-related challenges
- **Tier 4**: Executive escalation for critical risks requiring organizational intervention

This monitoring framework ensures that risk treatments remain effective in the face of evolving threats and organizational changes, while maintaining compliance with ISO 27001 Clause 9.1 (Monitoring, measurement, analysis and evaluation) and Clause 10 (Continual improvement).
