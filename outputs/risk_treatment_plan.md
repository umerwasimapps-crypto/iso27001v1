## Monitoring and Follow-up

### **Progress Monitoring**
- **Weekly Status Reviews**: Critical risk treatments (30-day timeline) will be reviewed weekly by the CISO and executive team
- **Monthly Progress Reports**: High-priority treatments (90-day timeline) will be reported monthly to the security management team
- **Quarterly Reviews**: Systemic treatments (6-12 month timeline) will be reviewed quarterly with all department heads

### **Effectiveness Measurement**
- **Control Validation**: Each implemented control will undergo technical validation testing to verify effectiveness
- **Risk Reassessment**: Risk scores will be recalculated after control implementation to measure risk reduction
- **Compliance Verification**: Regular audits against ISO 27001 Annex A requirements will verify control implementation

### **Continuous Improvement**
- **Lessons Learned**: Post-implementation reviews will capture lessons learned for continuous improvement
- **Control Optimization**: Implemented controls will be regularly reviewed and optimized based on operational experience
- **Threat Intelligence**: Ongoing threat intelligence will inform control adjustments and enhancements

### **Stakeholder Communication**
- **Customer Updates**: Regular security posture updates will be provided to key customers
- **Regulatory Reporting**: Compliance status will be reported to regulatory bodies as required
- **Executive Dashboard**: Executive-level dashboard will provide real-time visibility into treatment progress and effectiveness

### **Escalation Procedures**
- **Critical Path Delays**: Any treatment falling behind schedule by more than 10% will be escalated to executive management
- **Effectiveness Issues**: Controls not achieving expected risk reduction will trigger immediate review and remediation
- **Resource Constraints**: Resource limitations preventing timely implementation will be escalated for resolution

This treatment plan provides a structured approach to risk reduction with clear ownership, timelines, and monitoring mechanisms. The plan balances immediate technical controls with foundational process improvements to achieve sustainable security posture improvement and ISO 27001 compliance.
