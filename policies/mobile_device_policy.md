# Mobile Device Policy (Demo)

## Purpose
Protect company data accessed via laptops, tablets, and phones.

## Scope
All devices enrolled in the Mobile Device Management (MDM) platform.

## Policy Statement
Devices must use disk encryption, screen locks, and approved VPN connections. Jailbroken or rooted devices are prohibited.

## Roles & Responsibilities
- IT Operations: enforce MDM profiles.
- Employees: report lost/stolen devices immediately.

## Compliance
Non-compliance can trigger remote wipe and disciplinary review.
