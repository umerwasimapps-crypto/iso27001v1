# Access Control Policy (Demo)

## Purpose
Define how access to information assets is granted, reviewed, and revoked.

## Scope
All employees, contractors, and systems connecting to company resources.

## Policy Statement
Access shall follow least privilege, role-based provisioning, and multi-factor authentication for privileged accounts.

## Roles & Responsibilities
- IT Operations: provision/deprovision accounts.
- Security Operations: review privileged activity logs.
- Department Managers: approve access requests.

## Compliance
Violations may result in access revocation and disciplinary action.
