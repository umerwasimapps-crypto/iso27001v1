# Incident Response Policy (Demo)

## Purpose
Provide a structured approach for detecting, responding to, and learning from security incidents.

## Scope
All information systems, personnel, and third parties handling company data.

## Policy Statement
Incidents follow phases: detection, triage, containment, eradication, recovery, post-incident review.

## Roles & Responsibilities
- Security Operations Center: monitor alerts and lead response.
- Communications: manage stakeholder updates.
- IT Operations: implement containment and recovery steps.

## Compliance
Incident reports must be filed within 24 hours of detection and archived for audits.
